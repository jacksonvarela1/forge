Week

Corner Notes

01
The skeleton never moves. Mon kicks, Tue combos, Wed boxing, Thu output, Fri counters, Sat hands, Sun restore. Built around your Wed/Sat squats. Only the content changes week to week.
02
Volume is capped, density is not. 7 MMA days on 4-5 lifts is your ceiling. Weeks get harder, never longer. Same 40 min, more packed in.
03
Tap any drill. The how-to now lives under it: steps, the one fix, and the exact video search. Moves tab keeps the full library.
04
Gear is trimmed to essentials. Gloves and mirror: owned. Wraps now, bag hunted or ordered week 4. Nonessentials are labeled exactly that.
05
Grass, not concrete. July pavement runs 140F+. It is a burn and a blister, and it grabs your foot mid-pivot and torques the knee.
06
The timer runs the session. It calls every 30 second station on conditioning days, names each round on skill days, and shows what is next. The double arrow skips ahead.
07
Autoregulate, do not white-knuckle. Ten weeks with no deload on top of 4-5 lifts is real. If your squat drops two sessions running, or you wake up stiff three days straight, cut Thu and Sat to 3 rounds for a week. Skill days stay. That is the deload, and taking it is not falling behind.
08
Screen and speaker. The screen stays awake while the timer runs. Connect your Bluetooth speaker first, then hit Start, and the coach follows it. If you cannot hear the coach, check the ring silent switch. If the phone does lock anyway, unlocking snaps the timer to exactly where you should be and the coach tells you.
09
On kicking volume. Capped before the bag on purpose, because your legs squat twice a week. The teep primer now opens Tue and Fri, Tuesday chains kicks into every combo, and from week 5 the bag pulls kicking toward half the work.
